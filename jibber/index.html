Pitahaya
Pitahaya is like a cable TV scrambler, but for Internet video. You can transmit different kinds of media (audio/video) over a scrambled video signal, and the signal can be decoded in real time directly in the browser with a bookmarklet. It has support for optional password-based encryption.
